In 2020 the International Conference on Unmanned Aircraft Vehicles will be held for first time outside of the US, in practice in Athens (Greece).

These conferences approaches the issues of integration of manned-unmanned aviation into the national airspace, legal, ethical and privacy issues, regulations, benefits of unmanned aviation to society, UAS/RPAS design for safety, reliability and resilience, and technology standards.  Every year many groups including funding agencies, academia and industry assist to ICUAS to discuss issues related to unmanned aircraft systems and their applications.

Important dates

February 14, 2020:  Full Papers/ Invited Papers/Tutorial Proposals Due

April 15, 2020: Acceptance/Rejection Notification 

May 8, 2020: Final, Camera Ready Papers; Early Registration Deadline

Find out more information about the call for papers at: http://www.uasconferences.com/2020_icuas/call-for-papers/

Also, you may find out more information about the conference in the ICUAS’ main page: http://www.uasconferences.com/2020_icuas/
